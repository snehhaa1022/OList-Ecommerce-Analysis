# 📊 Olist E-commerce Dashboard Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the **Olist E-commerce Dataset** using multiple Business Intelligence and Data Analytics tools. The objective was to uncover customer purchasing trends, evaluate logistics performance, analyze payment behavior, and derive actionable business insights.

The project includes dashboards built using:

- Microsoft Excel
- Tableau
- Power BI
- SQL

---

## 🎯 Project Objective
The main goals of this analysis were to:

- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Analyze sales trends and performance
- Evaluate delivery efficiency
- Study payment patterns
- Measure customer satisfaction levels
- Generate business insights through interactive dashboards

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The project tracks multiple KPIs, including:

- Total Orders
- Distinct Customers
- Sales Analysis
- Delivery Performance
- Customer Satisfaction
- Payment Trends
- Shipping Time Analysis

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- **Total Orders Analyzed:** 112.59K+
- **Distinct Customers:** 94.5K+
- **Domain:** E-commerce

The dataset was analyzed to identify trends in:

- Customer behavior
- Order patterns
- Delivery timelines
- Review scores
- Payment preferences

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 1. Weekday vs Weekend Sales Analysis
A majority of payments occurred during weekdays, showing stronger business activity Monday to Friday.

### 2. Payment & Customer Satisfaction
Payment trends were analyzed alongside review scores to understand customer experience.

### 3. Pet Shop Delivery Performance
Delivery efficiency was studied for specific product categories to identify logistical improvements.

### 4. São Paulo Customer Spending Behaviour
Regional customer spending habits were analyzed for market insights.

### 5. Shipping Time vs Review Score
Faster deliveries showed a strong correlation with higher customer ratings.

---

## 💡 Key Takeaways

### 🚚 Logistics Drives Loyalty
Fast shipping directly impacts customer satisfaction and review scores. Reducing shipping times can significantly improve customer experience.

### 📢 Strategic Marketing
With **77.2% of payments occurring on weekdays**, marketing efforts should focus more heavily on weekday promotions.

### 📊 Dashboard-Driven Decisions
Using **Excel, Tableau, and Power BI** transformed raw data into meaningful insights, enabling better business decisions.


## 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how business intelligence tools can transform raw e-commerce data into actionable insights. By analyzing customer behavior, logistics performance, and sales trends, businesses can improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
